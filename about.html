About Us

Background Story

Here at Dhruv's Kitchen, we've been serving authentic cuisines for over a decade. Established by Chef Jay in 2008, our restaurant has won numerous awards including the 'Best Fine Dining Experience' in 2020.

Our restaurant is located in the heart of the city and is easily accessible by public transport. We are open from 10am to 10pm daily.

Our restaurant is also available for private events such as weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. We have a private dining room that can accommodate up to 50 people.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the best dining experience possible. We strive to provide the highest quality food and service at an affordable price.

Our Team

Our team consists of 3 members, each with their own unique skills and talents. We are all passionate about food and we hope to share our passion with you.

Chef dhruv

Chef dhruv, also Head Chef, is the founder of dhruv's Kitchen. He has over 20 years of experience in the culinary industry and has worked in many restaurants around the world. He is also a MasterChef winner.

Pastry Chef

Pastry Chef is the pastry chef of dhruv's Kitchen. He has over 10 years of experience in the culinary industry and has worked in many restaurants around the world. He is also a MasterChef winner.

Junior Chef

Junior Chef is the junior chef of dhruv's Kitchen. He has over 5 years of experience in the culinary industry and has worked in many restaurants around the world. He is also a MasterChef winner.
